How do I handle out-of-stock items?

Offer best-match substitutions and confirm via SMS. If a guest declines, remove the item and note the conversation. When in doubt, use the substitution ladder in the playbook.

Can I refund individual items?

Yes. Use the partial refund flow in Shopper app ‚Üí Order Actions ‚Üí Adjustments. Document the reason and confirm the updated total with the guest.

What if delivery is running late?

Send a proactive text with the new ETA, apologize, and offer a make-good (delivery fee waive or $5 credit). Follow the late-delivery script to keep the guest informed.

Who do I contact for POS or payment failures?

Escalate to the Digital Ops bridge and open a ticket in ServiceNow. Include store number, order ID, payment type, and screenshots when possible.
