Military time quick chart

Progression to translate standard to military time at a glance.

Add 1200 after noon

12:00 PM becomes 1200, then keep adding 100 for every hour after.

Mind midnight vs. noon

12:00 AM = 0000 (midnight). 12:00 PM = 1200 (noon).

Leading zero in the morning

Use a zero before 1:00–9:00 AM (e.g., 7:00 AM → 0700).

DC credit reminders

  1. Issue in-store credit, not a refund.
  2. Redeemable only in-store; not curbside or delivery.
  3. Credit will not appear in the account or trigger messages.
  4. Receipt shows it as "Store Value."
  5. Expires in 21 days at the exact issue time.
  6. Wait 24 hours before using the credit.
  7. Redeem with a cashier (not self-checkout or scan-and-go).
