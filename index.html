    Digital coupons

    Steps for DC
    1. Confirm the customer clipped the coupon during the CSR transition using the customer login link.
    2. Check My clipped coupons. If it's an in-store purchase and the amount is above $50, ask L2 for a BI report and transfer.
    3. Log in to Logix using your OnePass username.
    4. Go to Customer > Inquiry, choose Advanced under the Household ID search bar, and search by email.
    5. Select the purple link.
    6. In Adjustments, enter the credit amount and include the coupon details as the explanation (copy and paste).
    7. Submit.
    Maria FP
    DC credit reminders
    1. Issue in-store credit, not a refund.
    2. Redeemable only in-store; not curbside or delivery.
    3. Credit will not appear in the account or trigger messages.
    4. Receipt shows it as "Store Value."
    5. Expires in 21 days at the exact issue time.
    6. Wait 24 hours before using the credit.
    7. Redeem with a cashier (not self-checkout or scan-and-go).
