Online Account Services Team (OAST)

OAST partners help customers navigate heb.com and the My H-E-B app—everything from account access to payment and checkout support.

Mission checklist

  • Confirm identity with two data points before reviewing account details.
  • Document device type (web/app), browser/app version, and screenshots when possible.
  • Use plain-language steps and stay on the line while customers test solutions.

Common requests

  • Password resets or MFA troubleshooting on heb.com and My H-E-B.
  • Account recovery when login is blocked or email is outdated.
  • Payment method updates, wallet errors, or digital coupon issues.

support_agent Call guides

Access & authentication

  1. Verify customer with name + phone or email tied to the profile.
  2. Check known outages in Salesforce/CSR before troubleshooting.
  3. For password or MFA issues, send Okta self-service reset link and confirm receipt.
  4. Walk through browser/app cache clear, then retry login with a fresh One-Time PIN.
  5. Escalate to Level 2 if repeated MFA failures persist after cache clear and device swap.

Ordering & checkout

  1. Identify whether the customer is using the app or web and confirm version/build.
  2. Have the customer toggle Wi-Fi/cellular or try an alternate browser (Chrome/Edge).
  3. For payment declines, confirm card expiration, ZIP, and available balance; retry after removing/re-adding card.
  4. For digital coupon application issues, reclip and verify eligibility in CSR before issuing credit.
  5. Escalate with screenshots, steps to reproduce, and timestamps if the error persists.

library_books Knowledge center

Documentation

Account recovery checklist
  1. Validate identity with two unique data points.
  2. Send password reset or MFA re-enrollment link via verified email/phone.
  3. Ask the customer to retry in a private/incognito window.
  4. Remove stale sessions in CSR if sign-in loops continue.
Payment and wallet stability
  • Re-add the payment method after confirming billing ZIP and CVV.
  • Advise customers to avoid VPNs/ad-blockers during checkout.
  • Have the customer refresh the cart, then place a fresh test order.

Digital coupons

Coupon troubleshooting
  1. Confirm coupon eligibility and clip status in CSR.
  2. Have the customer reclip and refresh the cart or app session.
  3. If not applying, issue in-store credit per policy and document in the case.
Escalation snapshot
  • Include customer ID, device type, app/web version, and steps to reproduce.
  • Add timestamp, order number (if available), and full error text.
  • Attach screenshots before sending to Level 2.
